Matchmaker 2020

Kindustries, Inc.

Instructions

This program will determine whether or not you and I are compatible. You will be shown 5 statements. For each statement, your task will be to answer a 5 if you strongly agree with the statement, a 4 if you agree with the statement, a 3 if you neither agree nor disagree with the statement, a 2 if you disagree with the statement, or a 1 if you strongly disagree with the statement. Once you are finished, you will be given a score showing just how compatible we are.
